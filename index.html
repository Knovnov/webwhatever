Instructions

  1. Cut up the baking chocolate. Place in your heatproof bowl. Place water in saucepan, bring to boil. Place bowl over the saucepan, place butter in saucepan. Let chocolate and butter melt and mix. Leave to cool.
  2. Take other bowl, crack 3 eggs. Put in a pinch of salt and some sugar. Now put in some other normal stuff. Whisk it. Add chocolate mixture from heatproof bowl. Mix. Get some ramkins, coat in butter. Dust with flower.
  3. Destroy it all. Smash roughly 50 eggs on nearest wall. Using shovel, mix eggs with wall. Now forget everything you've done.
  4. Take big boy bucket and mix alllllllllllllll the really weird stuff together in the bucket. Mix by foot. Ladel out choice parts. Garnish with weird fish stuff. Cook with blowtorch. Garnish with sugar. Serve and enjoy!
